Manhattan

Description

A timeless classic from the late 1800s

Ingredients

  • 50ml Rittenhouse rye Whiskey
  • 20ml Antica Formula vermouth
  • 5ml Lillet Blanc
  • 2 dash Angostura bitters

How do I make it?

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice
  2. Stir for 30 seconds
  3. Strain into a frozen glass
  4. Garnish with a brandied cherry
